Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Quotation Management System? +
Our system helps businesses create, manage, and track quotations efficiently with an intuitive web-based interface.
Can I export quotations to PDF? +
Yes! You can generate professional PDF quotations instantly with your company’s branding and share them with clients.
Is there a way to track client responses? +
Absolutely. The system provides status tracking so you can see whether a client has accepted, declined, or is reviewing a quotation.
Can multiple users access the system? +
Yes, you can manage user roles and give access to team members with proper permissions.
Is my data secure? +
We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to ensure your quotations and client data remain safe.
